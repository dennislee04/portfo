03 : About me

A little intro:

I was born in Manitowac (Wisconson), in the year of 1990. I'm a 1st Generation of Hmong descent to be born in the USA. However, like most of the highly populated Hmong cities today, I was raised in the Twin Cities (Saint Paul/Minneapolis), my whole life.

IT Career Timeline:

In October 2013, I started as an "IT Paraprofessional Student Worker" for MNIT Services@Minnesota Manage & Budget(MMB), working on the HelpDesk/Desktop team. Gradly expanding on my knowledge, I was offered a full time "ITS2 Service Desk Analyst" position in April 2015, after my predecessor went on to explore new opportunities.
